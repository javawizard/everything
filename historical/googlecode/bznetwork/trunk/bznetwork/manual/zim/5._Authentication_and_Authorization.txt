====== 5. Authentication ======
Created Saturday 22 August 2009

BZNetwork has a sophisticated authentication and authorization system. BZNetwork can authenticate users based on their BZFlag callsign or a BZNetwork-specific username and password. BZNetwork can authorize users based on individual permissions applied to particular items.
